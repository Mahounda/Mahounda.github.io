Biography

Mahounda Poinsonnet is a Senior Portfolio Coordinator with eight years of experience in fast paced technology environment, specialising in Financial Management, Reporting, and Governance. She is now transitioning into hybrid PMO, Tech & Data roles, supported by hands on development experience gained through the She Codes program. Mahounda has built projects using Python, Django, JavaScript, React, HTML, and relational concepts, and naturally stepped into the Project Manager/Scrum Master role during her final group project, guiding her team to deliver a complete application. She thrives in environments that value curiosity, continuous learning, and collaborative problem-solving. Beyond her technical journey, she is engaged in her community, participating in local community dinners, contributing to the next French councillor elections and enjoying meaningful family times. Embracing life as an “électron libre” she brings energy, adaptability, and creativity to every step of her growing career in tech
